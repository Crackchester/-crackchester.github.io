What is Crackchester?

Crackchester is a society formed by current students of the University of Manchester. We are dedicated to crack cyber security and mostly take part in security competitions. We also aim to educate people on the technical apsecs of cyber security by publishing writeups and holding workshops.

What do these competitions look like?

These competitions are mostly CTFs.

Capture The Flag (CTF) is a computer security wargame, where each participating individual or team competes to find the flag (key) by solving problems or hacking the prepared boxes. Usually, it involves many security fields, such as web hacking, binary reverse engineering, exploitation, forensics, cryptography, etc.

Why should I join Crackchester?

We provide you with an environment of people who are passionate about cyber security and have practical experience thanks to the many competitions we take part in. If you would like to learn more about security by taking part in these competitions or getting involved in security related projects then you are more than welcome to join us.

How can I join?

You can join by using the student unions website: https://manchesterstudentsunion.com/groups/crackchester
